Game Controls

WASD/ Arrows: movement

J/ Z/ Spacebar: jump

K/ X: run

Tile Editor Controls

Access tile editor by holding "e" after refresh.

Left Click: draw current tile

Right Click: delete tile

Space Bar: Move Viewport

A/ D: switch current tile

S: switch tool

Selection tool

  • Hold left click and drag to make selection
  • Left click again to drag selection
  • Right click to finish and paste selected tiles
